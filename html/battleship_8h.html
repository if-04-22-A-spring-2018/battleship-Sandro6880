Enumeration Type Documentation

enum CellContent

The values assigned to each cell of the players, the opponents and the guesses field. The player's and the opponent's field only hold the values Water or Boat. The guesses field may also hold Unknowns. If a reference of a shot or a guess (pair of row/col) is outside the field the value OutOfRange is used.
